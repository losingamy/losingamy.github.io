Wild Hare Acres

Bringing us closer to the origin of our food in a modern era.

Our Farm

We currently operate out of our surburban home, raising rabbits and vegetables in our backyard garden. We started as a way to become more self-sustainable and because we find modern commerical farming to be barbaric. We treat our animals with care and respect before they serve their purpose as meals.

Rabbits

We raise various breeds of rabbits on our farm. Our breeding stock currently consists of four New Zealands, one TAMUK, and one Californian. We do not sell live rabbits for any reason at this time. If you are interested in a pet rabbit, please visit Magic Happens Rescue to find a perfect hoppy friend for you.

Vegetables

We aim for organic gardening as much as possible, not using harsh pesticides or herbicides in any of our raised beds. We feel it is not in the best interest of our health or the health of our animals to use these products. We currently source our seeds from various vendors including Burpee and MIGardener.

Goals

We look to learn and grow from every experience. Farming comes with its successes and failures and while we can always fall back on store-bought items, we aim to harvest wisdom most of all. We are currently working on projects to improve the growth rate of our rabbits, provide the community with heirloom seedlings you won't find in the nurseries, and saving to move to a few acres.
